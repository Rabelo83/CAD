## Fit & Interface Notes
- MagSafe puck press-fits into pocket (snug, not glued)
- Cable routes through channel to rear/bottom
- No phone lip needed — MagSafe magnet holds phone
- Supports both portrait and landscape orientation (version B)

## Print Settings (Flashforge AD5X)
- **Max build**: 220 x 220 x 220mm — FITS (130 x 130 x 128mm)
- Material: PLA (stone/sand color for rock look)
- Colors: 4-color — (1) stone body, (2) gold cross, (3) brown book, (4) gold coin tray
- Layer height: 0.2mm
- Nozzle: 0.4mm
- Infill: 20%
- Supports: YES (for MagSafe pocket overhang)
- Orientation: Base down (upright)
